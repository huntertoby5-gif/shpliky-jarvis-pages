Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-04

JARVIS runs entirely on hardware owned by its operator ("Shpliky") and is used only to manage the operator's own social media accounts. It does not collect, store, share, or sell personal data belonging to any third party.

The only account data JARVIS handles is the operator's own TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram authentication tokens, which are stored locally on the operator's device and are never transmitted to any third party or external service besides the platform APIs (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram/Meta) they belong to.

Questions: contact the owner directly.
